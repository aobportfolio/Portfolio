About

I am an artist-scholar working at the intersection of experimental theatre practice and performance theory. As co-founder and Artistic Director of Roaming Gnome Theatre Company, I've directed over 24 productions since 2022, developing a unique approach that brings avant-garde techniques to youth ensemble work.

My practice encompasses directing, costume design, sound design, and dramaturgy, always informed by theoretical inquiry. I hold a dual BA in Theatre and Political Science from Trinity University, where I was deeply influenced by Berlin's experimental theatre tradition through intensive study of German theatre movements.
